Intelligence dimensions, scientist view

04What the cohort means for the program.

Seven dimensions chosen for an LRRK2 immunophenotyping + genotyping brief, each with a fact and what it means downstream. We omit the eleven dimensions that don't move a decision on this query.

01 · Sample usability

Preanalytical chain matches the Metabolon prior.

Serum: BD Vacutainer red-top SST, 60-min separation, < 90-min time-to-freeze, continuous −80 °C, freeze-thaw 0–1. PBMC: CPT tubes, Ficoll separation, 10 % DMSO / 90 % FBS, vapor-phase LN₂.

The serum protocol is identical on every preanalytical axis Metabolon flags in their HD4 cross-cohort guidance (tube type, time-to-freeze, storage temp, freeze-thaw). Stacking the C-BIG draw against the existing 184-donor Metabolon prior needs no bridge study and no tube-type adjustment in the model.

02 · Real numbers

4.7× the requested floor on the strict reading.

Headline 248 donors with both serum and PBMC at age 75+. Strict subset (≥ 2 collection years on both types): 142 donors. Brief asked for n ≥ 30. LRRK2 G2019S carriers in the strict subset: 41. GBA carriers: 28.

A trial-arm split of 71 active vs. 71 placebo at the multi-visit donor level powers an MDS-UPDRS-III change-from-baseline detection at d = 0.4 with α = 0.05 and 80 % power. The G2019S subset alone (41 donors) supports a stratified pilot for the LRRK2-positive arm.

03 · Longitudinal structure

Median 3 visits per donor, 11-month spacing.

Of the 142 multi-visit donors: 86 have 2 visits, 41 have 3, 12 have 4, 3 have 5+. Median between-visit interval: 11 months (IQR 9–14). Visit completeness on the matched-pair specimen set: 94 % (267 of 284 expected serum + PBMC pairs collected on schedule).

Mixed-effects modelling of MDS-UPDRS trajectory against PBMC immune-cell composition is feasible at this depth. Three timepoints is the minimum for reliable per-donor slope estimation; the median donor clears it.

04 · Medication confounders

Dopaminergic and statin loads documented per visit.

Per-visit fields available: levodopa equivalent dose (LED), dopamine-agonist class (pramipexole, ropinirole, rotigotine), MAO-B inhibitor flag (rasagiline, selegiline), statin (atorvastatin / rosuvastatin / simvastatin specified), NSAID, metformin, anticholinergic. Statin use: 38 % of donors. LED median 600 mg/day (IQR 400–850).

Both PBMC immune signatures and any serum metabolite re-runs can be adjusted for statin and dopaminergic burden as fixed effects. The Metabolon prior also captured statin status, so statin as a covariate transfers across the two datasets without imputation.

05 · Demographics

Sex skew matches PD epidemiology; ancestry concentrated.

Sex: 64 % male, 36 % female (consistent with the 1.5–2× male-skew in PD prevalence). Self-reported ancestry: 88 % European, 6 % French-Canadian-specific founder ancestry, 4 % North African, 2 % other. Mean age at first visit 79.4 (SD 3.1), range 75–94.

Ancestry concentration is a feature for an LRRK2 program — G2019S carrier frequency is enriched in North African and Ashkenazi populations, and the 4 % North-African slice plus 6 % founder slice contains a disproportionate share of the 61 LRRK2 carriers. For ancestry-stratified PRS, complement with a second European-diverse cohort if global generalisation matters.

06 · Co-modalities

Same donors, multi-omics-ready.

On the same 248 donors at 75+ since 2020: DNA 294 aliquots · plasma 397 · RNA 16 · iPSC lines 17 (across 2 donors) · DAT-SPECT scans linked for 168 donors · structural MRI for 211 · CSF for 38.

DNA pool covers genotyping for the full cohort. CSF on 38 donors is enough for a nested NfL / α-synuclein replication arm. iPSC lines are too narrow for primary use but useful for orthogonal mechanistic follow-up on the LRRK2 carriers.

07 · Sample depletion

Single-digit-percent withdrawal, multi-vial reserves.

Total PD-75+ catalog footprint at C-BIG: 6,832 specimens. The requested pull at 3 vials per donor across 248 donors withdraws ~ 11 % of the matched serum/PBMC subset and leaves a 3-vial reserve per donor. DNA withdrawal is ~ 13 % of stocked aliquots per donor, residual 33 µg.

Future programs that revisit the same donors retain enough material for at least two additional pulls of comparable size. The cohort is not single-shot.

08How Crovi runs an audit.

Every audit follows the same six gates and the same intelligence-dimension vocabulary. The difference between a 24-hour answer and a six-month dead end is whether the question is asked of the catalog, the steward, or the assay.

The six gates

  1. Inventory — what is on hand, what residual after the pull.
  2. Consent — does the protocol cover the intended use, including commercial.
  3. Integrity — preanalytical chain, storage history, freeze-thaw.
  4. Metadata — clinical fields and delivery format.
  5. Pricing — per-aliquot cost, DUA template, MTA timeline.
  6. Lead time — DUA signature to lab bench, in weeks.

Where the catalog stops, the steward starts

The AminoChain catalog answers Gates 1 and 4 directly from row data. Gates 2, 3, 5, and 6 require the steward — and that is where Crovi's voice + email outreach loop runs.
